Final project ideas

  • Convert a non-digital or inaccessible source into open data

    • Geo-referencing a historic map

    • Tracing features from a geo-referenced historic map

    • Extracting a table from a PDF

    • Georeference an image map

  • Evaluate and document existing open data source or standard

  • Document a reproducible workflow for open data

  • Contribute to a real data public

    • Report issues (e.g. damaged access ramps, out street lights, trash) to Baltimore 311 and track responses

    • Contribute edits or new features to Open Street Map

    • Request and share data from a public agency using MPIA or FOIA

  • Explore data about you and your community

    • Analyze data you share through social media or location tracking services
